Active Thermal Control Loop

Objective: Maintain satellite internal component temperatures within a $\pm 2\text{K}$ stability window during orbital solar flux transitions.

1. Mathematical Foundations

The system is modeled using a transient energy balance:

$$m C_p \frac{dT}{dt} = Q_{solar} + Q_{gen} - \epsilon \sigma A (T^4 - T_{space}^4)$$

2. Control Logic & Implementation

A PID controller was implemented to modulate the pump mass flow rate $\dot{m}$. The control law is defined as:

$$u(t) = K_p e(t) + K_i \int e(t) dt + K_d \frac{de(t)}{dt}$$
